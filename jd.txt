SENIOR BACKEND ENGINEER – Stripe (Remote)

You'll work closely with product and platform teams to ship reliable, scalable payment solutions.

- Own end-to-end development of APIs and microservices in a distributed systems environment
- Drive technical design reviews and architecture decisions
- Improve observability, monitoring, and alerting for production systems
- Mentor junior and mid-level engineers on best practices
- Collaborate with security and compliance teams to ensure PCI-DSS compliance

Requirements:
- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python or Java
- Deep experience with distributed systems and microservices architecture
- Hands-on experience with AWS (ECS, Lambda, DynamoDB, SQS) or equivalent cloud platforms
- Proficiency with relational databases (PostgreSQL) and NoSQL databases
- Experience building and consuming RESTful APIs at scale
- Familiarity with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes) and CI/CD pipelines
- Strong understanding of system design, scalability, and performance optimization
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Nice to Have:
